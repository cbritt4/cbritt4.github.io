About me

I’m an astrophysics postdoctoral researcher in Jay Strader’s group. I’m interested in the population and evolution of low mass binaries (LMXBs), especially at low luminosities. I also am interested in multiwavelength follow-up of unassociated gamma-ray sources discovered by the Fermi Gamma-ray Observatory. A summary of my research is avilable here.
